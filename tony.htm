His current research interests include computer graphics, non-photorealistic rendering, image-based rendering, visualization, virtual reality, surgical simulation, medical visualization and medical system, and distributed and collaborative virtual environments. He also serves on the editorial boards of the IEEE Transactions on Visualization and Computer Graphics (TVCG) (2022/09), the IEEE Transactions on Information Technology in Biomedicine, the Visual Computer, the Computers and Graphics Journal,  the Journal Recent Patents on Engineering, the Journal on Information Science and Engineering, and the Journal of Software Engineering. He served as a member of the international program committees of several conferences including IEEE Visualization, Pacific Graphics (Program Co-chair for PG2011), the IEEE Pacific Visualization Symposium, IEEE Virtual Reality, the IEEE-EMBS International Conference on Information Technology and Applications in Biomedicine, the International Conference on Artificial Reality and Telexistence, and the International Conference in Central Europe on Computer Graphics, Visualization, and Computer Vision. He is a senior member of the IEEE and a member of the ACM.
